Steps for cooking a delicious Lasagna dish at home

Things Required: 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1/4-inch strips Half cup lime juice 2 (1 ounce) packets taco seasoning mix (such as Old El PasoÂ®) 1and half cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided 1 cup sour cream 1 cup salsa salt and ground black pepper to taste 1 (8 ounce) package no-boil lasagna noodles, or to taste

Description:(Stepwise instructions for preparation)

  • Step 1 : Combine chicken, lime juice, and taco seasoning in a large bowl. Stir to make sure chicken is evenly covered.
  • Step 2 : Cook chicken mixture over medium heat until firm, about 15 minutes.
  • Step 3 : Mix 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, sour cream, and salsa in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper.
  • Step 4 : Put a few spoonfuls of the sour cream mixture in the bottom of a 9x9-inch baking dish. Layer noodles and chicken on top. Cover with remaining 1 cup mozzarella cheese.
  • Step 5 : Bake in the preheated oven until noodles are tender and cheese is melted, about 1 hour.
